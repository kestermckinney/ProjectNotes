Overview

Why ProjectNotes exists

ProjectNotes is designed around the actual rhythm of project delivery. Teams need to capture meeting notes, assign follow-up actions, track risks and issues, and keep reporting consistent without scattering information across disconnected tools.

The application gives project managers one place to manage those connected workflows, with room for organization-specific automation and reporting.

It combines a desktop application, a growing mobile experience, and plugin-driven automation so teams can tailor the product to the way they actually work.

ProjectNotes is especially useful when discipline, traceability, and regular follow-up matter more than generic collaboration features.
